About Our Seminar Library

The Seminar Library of the Department of Information and Communication Engineering (ICE), Rajshahi University, fosters academic excellence, research empowerment, and lifelong learning.

📜 Our History

Established in the early 2000s, the ICE Seminar Library began with a handful of journals and textbooks. Over the years, it has transformed into a vibrant academic hub offering both digital and physical resources. It now plays a crucial role in supporting research, seminars, and final-year thesis projects for students and faculty.

🎯 Mission & Vision

Our mission is to ensure open and equal access to a wide range of academic resources, promoting scholarly growth and innovation. Our vision is to establish a globally recognized library that equips students with the knowledge and tools needed for success in the ever-evolving tech world.

📚 Our Resources

Our library is stocked with thousands of curated textbooks, research articles, IEEE journals, project documentation, and digital tools. Students have access to digital databases like IEEE Xplore and ACM, computers for coding and research, and high-speed internet for uninterrupted learning.

🏛️ Library Environment

A calm, air-conditioned space with ergonomic seating, bright lighting, and friendly support staff. Group study zones and private reading corners provide flexibility. Whether working solo or with peers, the library ensures a supportive atmosphere for focus and creativity.
